Dog Grooming Services

We're committed to providing your dog with the best possible care, offering a wide selection of grooming services to keep them clean, well-groomed, and fashionable. Our professional groomers offer relaxing baths, tailored haircuts, nail care, and ear cleaning in a stress-free, peaceful setting. Trust us to give your furry friend the royal treatment they deserve!

Basic Bath and Brush

Get your furry friend refreshed with our basic service. We will gently give your pup a shampoo bath, brush, and blow dry. Our groomers will also trim their nails, give them an ear cleaning, and end the service with a dash of cologne leaving your dog feeling luxurious.

Full Grooming

Do you need more than just bath and nail trim? This full service gives your dog what is in the basic bath and brush package, plus haircut or breed-specific styling by professional groomers. You can also request teeth cleaning and de-shedding treatment!

Puppies First Groom

Providing a gentle introduction to grooming that makes your puppy’s experience fun and safe. With plenty of treats to reward good behavior, we ensure a positive atmosphere throughout the light bath, brushing, and nail trim. Our caring staff is dedicated to helping your puppy feel comfortable and confident!

DeShedding Treatment

Specialized service designed for long-haired and double-coated dogs, effectively reducing shedding at home. This treatment not only minimizes loose fur but also promotes a healthy, shiny coat. Let us help your furry friend look their best while keeping your home fur-free!

Deluxe Spa Package

Includes a full grooming service along with fabulous add-ons like deep conditioning, paw balm, and a refreshing blueberry facial to brighten your pet's coat. This pampering treatment leaves your furry friend feeling revitalized and looking their best. To top it off, each pet leaves with a stylish bandana or bow, adding a fashionable touch to their new look!

Service Add-Ons

Teeth Brushing

Our teeth brushing add-on promotes your pet’s dental health and freshens their breath. This quick service reduces plaque buildup, ensuring a brighter smile. Give your furry friend the gift of a healthy mouth with this essential enhancement!

Flea and Tick Treatment

Protects your pet from unwanted pests, ensuring their comfort and health. This quick application helps prevent infestations and keeps your furry friend safe during outdoor adventures.

Special Skin Treatment

Designed to soothe and nourish your dog’s skin, addressing issues like dryness and irritation. This targeted treatment promotes a healthy, vibrant coat while providing much-needed relief.

Paw and Nose Moisturizing

Hydrates and protects your dog's sensitive areas, keeping them soft and healthy. This quick application helps soothe dryness and cracking, ensuring your furry friend stays comfortable.
